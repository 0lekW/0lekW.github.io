olekViz

C++
JUCE

Smart audio visualisations for device audio, or attatched program.

My most recent project. It is an interactive Windows program to display clever and useful audio visuals
for music producers or bored music listeners.

Why did I make olekViz?

I started making olekViz in response to the growing trend of music producers posting short form content of their music production process on social media. The program is intended to be a visual display of the audio playing so that the video is more engaging and entertaining to watch.

What does this project contain?

olekViz is a work in progress, but so far..

  • FFT analysis of audio to produce Specturm Analyser Visual
  • Waveform Visual
  • Ability to listen to audio from entire system using WASAPI
  • Finding and capturing Windows processes
